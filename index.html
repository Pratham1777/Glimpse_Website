Mission & Vission

The vision behind Ananta was to create a unique identity of the university. Ananta is also a representation of the ideologies of the university which are inherited and initiated by the students of the university to create the ultimate event of the university once in a year. The aim of Ananta was to bring all the students from various disciplines on a common platform and bring the vibrancy back in the university after halts produced due to covid.

Ananta Days Ananta Days

INERTIA

Inertia was the name given to the technical events . These events represented SOT,SOM and SOS respectively. It was a hard core competition based on technical knowledge wrapped in the cover of small competitions .

EQUILIBRIUM

Equilibrium is the identity given to the series of guest lectures. We all were pleased to hear the words of wisdom from (list of speakers). The event regularly attracts a diverse range of attendees from around the world, across different professions, and with different levels of experience transform your business.

SWOOSH

Non-Technical events were represented by Swoosh. They were all fun based competitions where the overall knowledge and skills of an individual or a team are tested. They were a perfect combination of fun and learning.

KALAKRITI

The workshop organised during Ananta goes by the name of kalakriti. These include workshops on tie-dye ,Candle making, Ar/VR effects etc. The aim of organising such workshops was to maintain the learning perspective of the fest.
